## monopoly

originally race to last one standing
originally written by a socialist as mockery of capitalism
also satired prison system
henry george: argued that we should only tax land not income
argues that that most taxes capitalists $\therefore$ they should pay most tax
technological progress -> improvements in land -> higher land value -> more rent
does not increase wages

lizzie magie took those ideas and made *Landlord's Game* 1904
satire of property ownership
played in colleges, informally published
1917 college students made a version called *Monopoly*, named after NJ town
Magie convinced major publisher to publish it
similar time, Charles Daro proposes similar game to dodge copyright
